<hr>
<p>Shortlisted applicants will be informed of the selection date, time and
tasks via email from 29 June 2026 onwards.&nbsp; All shortlisted applicants
will be required to attend a selection trial / audition. &nbsp;If further
shortlisted, applicants will be required to attend an interview with a
School Leader.</p>
<p>&nbsp;All communication will be made via email, to the email address that
is indicated in the online DSA-Sec Portal. &nbsp;Please ensure that the
email address provided is accurate and that the email address is accessed
actively during this period.</p>
<p>&nbsp;Students who are successfully admitted to the school via DSA are
expected to honour their commitment to the school and participate in the
activities related to the talent they are selected for from year 1 to 4.</p>
<p>&nbsp;Swiss Cottage Secondary School reserves the right not to release
details of the shortlisting process, interview and selection trial to all
applicants. The school will release the results for all DSA applicants
(whether successful or not successful) <strong>tentatively </strong>within
the period 17 to 28 August 2026.</p>
